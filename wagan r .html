Wagon R Information

The Wagon R is a 5 seater 4 cylinder carand has length of 3655 mm, width of 1620 mm and a wheelbase of 2435 mm. Read More Standout Features of Maruti Wagon R 60:40 split-folding rear seat

Maruti Suzuki, in its endeavour to push forward the cause of green mobility, has launched the Maruti Suzuki WagonR CNG. The new WagonR CNG comes with a new body and design that still remains true to its original “tall-boy” stance. At its heart, the Maruti Suzuki WagonR CNG employs the S-CNG technology that provides a perfect balance of drivability and high fuel efficiency. S-CNG technology is available in WagonR LXI and VXI CNG variants. The factory fitted CNG kit in Maruti Suzuki WagonR CNG has a leak proof design thanks to the advanced components used, and contributes to a higher engine lifespan. Equipped with dual interdependent ECUs with Intelligent Injection System, WagonR CNG delivers superior performance that is characterized by smooth pickup and great drivability. The total CNG tank capacity in WagonR S-CNG stands at 60L (water equivalent).
